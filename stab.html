The Truth Revealed

You have severed the tie that held the Crimson Dawn together. The veil of illusions falls away, revealing the true purpose of the society: control, not power. The heart was not a symbol of strength, but of dominance, designed to bind those who sought meaning in the darkness.

You have broken free. But at what cost?

The Crimson Dawn crumbles, but in its wake, you are left with knowledge too heavy to bear. The truth, once revealed, is a burden you will carry for the rest of your days.

"The Crimson Dawn's purpose was never to guide. It was to subjugate."

The shadows recede, leaving you alone with the truth. There is no going back.
